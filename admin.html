Divisions for Competition

Select a competition, then add its age/gender divisions (e.g., U15 Men, U19 Women, Senior Men). You can also reorder divisions to control how they appear on the public page.

Scoring settings (optional). Used for the Calc button in Results to auto-fill points.
Time-based formula: points = base_points + (base_time - actual_time) × points_per_second
Fence formula: points = base_points + (victories - base_victories) × points_per_victory
Diff is always rounded down to match UIPM-style brackets.

Divisions

Athletes & Results

Select a competition and division, then add/edit athletes and enter results (raw & points) for each discipline. Handicaps auto-calculate from total points (leader = 0).
Tie-break: appears only after all Laser Run points are entered for the division.

Enter raw performance (e.g., "10V 5D", "2:18.50") and points for each discipline. Click Calc to auto-fill points using division scoring settings, then Save. Handicaps are recalculated automatically for the whole division on save (1 point = 1 second).
